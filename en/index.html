// faq

Frequently asked questions

Do you only work with large companies?

No. We build everything from MVPs to validate new business lines to mission-critical corporate systems. The process adapts to the size of the project.

Do you only build software?

Software is our focus, but we solve the whole technology problem: infrastructure, automation and operations.

Can you work with clients outside Chile?

Yes. We work remotely with clients in any country and are authorized to export development services. Contracts are signed under Chilean law.

Can you integrate AI into my system?

Yes. We integrate language models and AI automation into new or existing systems: smart document capture, internal assistants and automated workflows. Always reviewed by our team, with a focus on measurable results.

Do you issue invoices?

Yes, of course.

How much does a project cost?

It depends on the scope. After a free first conversation, we send you a clear proposal with stages, verifiable milestones and a fixed budget.

How long will my project take?

An MVP usually takes weeks; a full system, a few months. The proposal includes a realistic schedule with verifiable milestones.

What do I need to get started?

Just your idea or requirement. Write to us, we’ll talk it through and suggest the best path: prototype, MVP or full build.
