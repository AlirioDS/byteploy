// faq

Frequently asked questions

Do you work only with companies, or with individuals too?

Both. We build everything from MVPs and prototypes for founders and individuals to custom systems for established companies.

Can you work with clients outside Chile?

Yes. We work remotely with clients anywhere in the world and we are set up to export software development services. Contracts are governed by Chilean law.

Are you a registered company? Do you issue invoices?

Yes. Byteploy is a company incorporated in Chile, a First Category taxpayer, registered under computer programming activities (code 620100). We issue invoices, including for service exports.

How much does a project cost?

It depends on scope. After a free first conversation, we send you a clear proposal with stages, timeline and pricing.

How long will my project take?

An MVP usually takes weeks; a full system, a few months. Your proposal includes a realistic schedule with verifiable milestones.

What do I need to get started?

Just your idea. Write to us, we talk it through, and we propose the best path: prototype, MVP or full build.
